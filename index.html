Capabilities

End-to-end support—from schematic capture and PCB layout through NPI, low-to-mid volume builds, and lifecycle sustainment.

  • Design: requirements, DFM/DFT, Altium/OrCAD, SI/PI, FPGA/MCU firmware
  • Manufacturing: SMT/THT, conformal coat, potting, cable harness, box build, ESS
